About Earth Guardians

We are a global movement of individuals and organizations working together to protect and restore our planet.

Our Vision

Earth Guardians envisions a world where humans live in harmony with nature, where ecosystems thrive, and where future generations inherit a healthy, vibrant planet.

Founded in 2008, we've grown from a small local initiative to an international network with chapters in over 90 countries, uniting people from all walks of life in the common goal of environmental stewardship.

Our work is guided by science, powered by communities, and focused on creating lasting solutions to the environmental challenges we face today.
